The Unraveling

[ Written in 2019 ]


Slowly undo the sadness, not to overwhelm the senses.

Comb your fingers over every wound,

Remembering where each came from —

Every lie, betrayal, and cutting word whittled into you.

Understand they hurt because they hurt,

from a place of desperation

and reasons beyond your comprehension.

Let love and grace fill the empty spaces,

Then feel that heaving in your chest steady

to a shaky breath —
